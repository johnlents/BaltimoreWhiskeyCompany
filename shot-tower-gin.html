Shot Tower Gin

Baltimore’s liquor history is thoroughly drenched in rye whiskey and so is our Shot Tower Gin.

Rested in used rye whiskey barrels, our gin mellows its flavor profile and picks up the signature sweetness and spice of the most significant spirit of Baltimore’s past, rye whiskey. There’s never been a gin so suited to call Baltimore home, so pour a shot and toast the Shot Tower. Cheers.

A work of love by Sam and John Lents
