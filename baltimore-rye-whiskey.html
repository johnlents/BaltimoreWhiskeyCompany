Baltimore Rye Whiskey

Rest easy, whiskey drinkers. With a name like The Baltimore Whiskey Company, you can bet we’re getting our hands into all kinds of whiskey. What we’re NOT doing is rushing it out the door.

Whiskey requires a delicate touch, and we don’t want to sacrifice quality whiskey for speed. Even most of our non­whiskey products will be touched by a rye whiskey barrel, so enjoy some drinks and relax. Whiskey is coming.
